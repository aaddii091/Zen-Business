Many institutions care deeply about student wellbeing, but their support systems are often fragmented. Concerns are noticed late, context is scattered across teams, and students can struggle to find the right level of help at the right moment.

Zengarden is designed to reduce that friction. It helps surface useful insight earlier, connect students with guided human support, and make it easier for care teams to respond with confidence.

The goal is not more monitoring for its own sake. The goal is better timing, better coordination, and a healthier support experience for everyone involved.

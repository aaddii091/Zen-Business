Scaling student wellbeing support does not mean automating care away. It means giving the right people better tools so they can notice, understand, and respond more effectively.

Zengarden keeps people at the center of the process. Students reflect, mentors guide, and clinicians provide oversight where professional care is needed. Technology supports the flow, but trust still lives in the human relationship.

That is how support systems grow without losing their integrity.

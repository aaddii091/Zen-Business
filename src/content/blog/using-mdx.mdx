This space can be used for richer Zengarden editorial content, including research notes, institution-facing updates, and mixed media posts that combine writing with interface elements.

## Why this format?

This format supports long-form content while still letting the team embed components where needed. That makes it useful for case studies, product walkthroughs, and support resources that need more than plain text.

It gives Zengarden a flexible publishing surface without needing a separate content system for every kind of article.

## Example

Here is a simple embedded component example for a future Zengarden article or resource page.

import HeaderLink from '../../components/HeaderLink.astro';

<HeaderLink href="#" onclick="alert('clicked!')">
	Explore Zengarden
</HeaderLink>

## Editorial Direction

- Publish student wellbeing insights and institutional guidance.
- Share product updates framed around trust, care, and responsible deployment.
- Use embedded components sparingly to support clarity, not distract from the content.

Student insight only matters if it is handled responsibly. Schools need clarity, but students also need dignity, informed processes, and confidence that support systems are working for them rather than on them.

Zengarden is built with that balance in mind. We emphasize privacy-conscious design, role-appropriate visibility, and practical oversight that helps institutions act with care.

Responsible support is not just a feature. It is part of the foundation.
